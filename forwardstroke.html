Forward Stroke

An Important Start

11 September 2017

The forward stroke is a basic step that is usually the first step that is taught when paddle boarding. It is also of the easiest steps to learn. The key thing for this step is to make sure you have the right body posture and grips to perform the stroke. More information will be provided in the rest of the page.

Technique

  1. Stand on the board.
  2. Grip the paddle with one hand at the top of the paddle and the other hand towards the middle of the paddle.
  3. Start paddling on the side that's opposite from the hand that is on top of the paddle.
  4. Follow through with your stroke by pushing towards you in the water.

Materials

  • Paddleboard
  • Paddle
  • Attachable leash

Tips

  • Keep your knees bent while you are on the board to ensure the best workout you can get every time. (Bend your knees slightly)
  • Keep feet shoulder width apart.(Very Important)
