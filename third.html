Switching Sides

A New Technique

11 September 2017

Switching sides with the paddle at a good pace everytime will help you from not falling in, and it will make you go faster if you stay at a good pace. Switching sides becomes as you perform the steps. Getting used to it is very easy, it's just when you first it becomes a little awkward switching over your body.

Technique

  1. Grip the paddle. One hand in the middle of the paddle and one at the top.
  2. Hold the paddle in the water on an angle on one side of your body.
  3. Switch sides once you paddled on the one side, then switch your grip on the paddle (the other hand on top and the other in the middle of the paddle) and place the paddle on the other side of the board.

Materials

  • Paddleboard
  • Paddle
  • Attachable leash

Tips

  • Keep your knees bent while you are on the board to ensure the best workout you can get every time.
  • Keep feet shoulder width apart.(Very Important)
