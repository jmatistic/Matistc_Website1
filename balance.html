Balance

Key to Success

11 September 2017

Balance is a very important step in learning to paddle board. When paddle boarding, your weight has to be even without the board or you will fall in. Learning to balance can be a little hard if you perform the right in order to keep balance on the board. It is not that hard to get balance on the paddle board, but once you get the hang of it, it becomes pretty easy and it becomes a habit and you won't even feel you are balancing on the board.

Technique

  1. Step on to the paddle board.
  2. Find the center of the board.
  3. PLace your feet on the center of the board, with your feet shoulder width apart.
  4. Place the paddle on one side and paddle evenly on both sides.

Materials

  • Paddleboard
  • Paddle
  • Attachable leash

Tips

  • Keep your knees bent while you are on the board to ensure the best workout you can get every time.
  • Keep feet shoulder width apart.(Very Important)
